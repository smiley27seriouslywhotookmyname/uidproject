Our Story

Fictional Fate was born from a simple idea: what if you could discover which fictional character truly matches your personality? Our team of psychology enthusiasts and pop culture nerds came togetherto create scientifically-informed yet fun personality quizzes that connect you with characters from your favorite movies, books, and TV shows.

We combine elements of the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI), and our own proprietary algorithms to create accurate and entertaining character matches. Our mission is to help people better understand themselves through the lens of the characters they love.

1. Take Our Quizzes

Our carefully crafted questions analyze your personality traits, values, and decision-making style through engaging scenarios and choices.

2. Advanced Matching

Our algorithm compares your responses with detailed character profiles from hundreds of fictional works, analyzing 50+ personality dimensions.

3. Discover Your Match

Receive your character match along with a detailed personality analysis and compatibility breakdown that explains why you're matched.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are your quizzes?

Our quizzes are designed by psychology professionals and use scientifically validated frameworks. While they're meant to be fun, we take the accuracy of our personality assessments seriously. We continuously refine our algorithms based on user feedback and psychological research.

How do you create character profiles?

Our team analyzes each character's behavior, motivations, and personality traits across their entire story arc. We consult original source material, psychological analyses, and sometimes even input from the creators when available. Each profile goes through multiple rounds of review before being added to our system.

Can I suggest new quizzes or characters?

Absolutely! We love hearing suggestions from our community. You can email us at suggestions@fictionafate.com with your ideas. Our most popular requests often get prioritized for future quizzes.
